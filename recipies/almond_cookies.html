Almond Cookies

Ingredients

  • 375g peanut butter
  • 85g maple syrup
  • 150g almond flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100g chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Combine peanut butter and maple syrup in a bowl
  2. add vanilla extract, salt and chocolate chips and mix
  3. add almond flour and mix until evenly distributed
  4. form balls and place on a tray
  5. bake for 12-15 minutes at 350F
